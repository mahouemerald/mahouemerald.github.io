Travel to Japan

Japan is an island country in East Asia that's part of the Ring of Fire. It's five main islands are Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu, and Okinawa.

Things You Can Do

  • See Tokyo Tower
  • Taste Japanese foods
  • Visit hotels
  • Explore the cities

    • Japan has many cities to explore, especially it's capital city, Tokyo.
